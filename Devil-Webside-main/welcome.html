Welcome friend.
You have stumbled into the chaotic
lair of Love Ramiz,
a mad programmer, music lover,
and curious wanderer from West Bengal.

At this thin slice of cosmic time,
I roam between pixels and melodies,
crafting apps that scare,
delight, and challenge.

Alongside me is Devil, my loyal companion,
whose antics keep the shadows interesting.
Music, visuals, and untamed ideas flow endlessly here.

No rules, no limits—just experiments,
failures, explosions, and sparks of brilliance.

Neva quit. Neva die.
